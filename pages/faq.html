Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking a bay, getting in the door at any hour, and playing your best golf indoors.

Getting Started
What is 24/7 Golf?

24/7 Golf is Michigan's premier indoor golf experience. Every location features private simulator bays powered by Trackman iO — the same launch-monitor technology trusted by Tour professionals — and every location is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. No tee times, no weather, no waiting for daylight.

Do I need to be a good golfer to play?

Not at all. Trackman iO works for every skill level — first-timers can play fun games and challenges (like Bullseye) while experienced players drill down into ball speed, spin, carry and shot shape on every swing. It's a great outing for families, friends and leagues alike.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is per bay, per hour — not per person. Bring your friends and split one rate. Rates vary by location and time of day; as an example, Ludington is $35/hour per bay on weekdays before 5pm and $50/hour after 5pm and on weekends. See your location's page for exact rates and membership discounts.

Does it cost anything to join?

No — creating a 24/7 Golf account is free and takes about a minute. You only pay for the bay time you book. Paid memberships are optional and add discounts or free daily play. Create your free account here.

Booking & Access
How do I book a bay?

Create a free account, then book through the 24/7 Golf app (App Store and Google Play) or online. Booking and payment are handled securely through CourtReserve. Pick your location, pick your bay, pick your time — done.

How do I get in at 2am if nobody works there?

The 24/7 Golf app unlocks the door and your bay during your reservation — no staff visit required. Book any time, day or night, and the app is your key.

How far in advance can I book, and can I cancel?

Members can typically reserve up to 30 days in advance and cancel up to 4 hours before a reservation without penalty. Exact booking windows depend on your membership — details are shown in CourtReserve when you book.

How many people can share a bay?

Bays comfortably fit a small group, and since pricing is per bay you can bring friends at no extra cost for golf.

In the Bay
What is Trackman iO?

Trackman iO is a tour-grade, ceiling-mounted launch monitor that tracks every shot with radar-and-camera precision — ball speed, launch, spin, carry and shot shape. It powers full simulated rounds on 250+ world-famous courses (Augusta to St. Andrews), practice ranges, skills tests, and party games.

Do I need my own clubs?

Bring your own clubs if you have them — you'll hit real balls with your real equipment.

Do you have pickleball?

Yes — our Ludington location has indoor pickleball courts you can reserve by the hour, year-round. Pickleball memberships with deep hourly discounts are available.

Memberships & Gift Cards
Do I need a membership to play?

No. The free Player account lets you book and play at standard hourly rates. Paid memberships add perks like free daily play, discounted rates and multi-location benefits — see your location's page for what's offered there.

Can I buy a gift card?

Yes — 24/7 Golf Ludington gift cards are available on the Ludington page, delivered instantly by email and redeemable for bay time.

I have another question — how do I reach you?

Call us at 616.350.9482 or email info@24-7golf.com and we'll get you sorted out.
